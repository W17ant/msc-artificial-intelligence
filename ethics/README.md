# The Credit Algorithm - AI Ethics Interactive Game

An interactive choose-your-own-adventure exploring ethical challenges in AI-powered credit scoring.

## Overview

Step into the role of a data scientist at a fintech startup facing ethical dilemmas around algorithmic bias, transparency, and fairness in automated lending decisions.

## Play Online

**[Launch Game](https://aoneill.co.uk/MSC/ai-ethics-finance-scenario.html)**

## Features

- **Branching Narrative:** Your choices shape the story outcome
- **Ethical Frameworks:** Decisions evaluated through multiple lenses:
  - Utilitarianism (greatest good)
  - Deontology (duty-based ethics)
  - Virtue Ethics (character-based)
- **Real Dilemmas:** Based on actual challenges in AI/ML deployment
- **Shareable Endings:** Compare your ethical profile with others

## Themes Explored

- Algorithmic bias and discrimination
- Transparency vs. competitive advantage
- Individual harm vs. systemic benefit
- Accountability in automated decisions
- The limits of "fairness" in ML

## Technical

- Pure HTML/CSS/JavaScript
- No external dependencies
- Works offline
